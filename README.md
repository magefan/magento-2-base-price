[Magento 2 Base Price](https://magefan.com/magento-2-base-price) is a solution that allows you to create price units or use pre-built ones to offer transparent pricing in your store. The extension will automatically calculate unit prices based on settings. 

Comply with unit prices regulations in your country and offer the best shopping experience in your store with Magefan.

## Requirements
  * Magento Community 2.3.0 - 2.4.x (CE, EE, ECE, B2B)
        
## Benefits
* 5 pre-built price units
* Unit price templates for products and listing pages
* Unit prices on products, categories, search pages and related products
* Unlimited price units
* Assign price units per each product

## Features

### Use Ready-Made Units or Create Your Own

Magento 2 Base Price Extensions comes with 5 built-in measurement units. However, you can also create an unlimited number of your customer units based on: 

* Length
* Height
* Volume 
* Meterage and more

Then simply assign the unit to the product and specify the product amount. That’s it. The extension handles the rest and calculates the unit price at a specified rate.

### Show Unit Prices on Multiple Pages
  
Clear and transparent pricing simplifies the purchasing decision and sometimes even drives one. Thus, you need to display unit prices on multiple pages, not only products. 

The Magento 2 Base Price Extension by Magefan allows you to enable unit prices on products, categories, search pages and related products. 

Make sure your customers can see unit prices wherever they are in your store. Help them make thought-out purchases!

<p align="center">
  <img width="853" height="425" src="https://cm.magefan.com/mf_webp/png/media/wysiwyg/products/magento-2-base-price.webp">
</p>

### Tweak the Unit Price Templates

How you display unit prices in Magento matters too. So the Base Price Extension for Magento 2 allows you to define that. 

Tweak the default base price templates to suit your requirements or use the default one for both product and list pages.
